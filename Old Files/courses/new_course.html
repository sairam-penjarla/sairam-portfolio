This one-hour, self-paced course introduces ChatGPT — a conversational AI app by OpenAI that can process text, image, and audio inputs. Learn to create an account, craft your first prompts, and explore practical, creative applications. Ideal for beginners with no prior AI experience.

  • Prompt Engineering: Learn to design prompts that yield clear and accurate AI results.
