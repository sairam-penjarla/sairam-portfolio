AI Project Timeline

A comprehensive roadmap for AI engineer projects, outlining key phases from concept to deployment and maintenance.

Scoping

Define project goals, objectives, and success metrics.

Solution Architecture

Design the system architecture, including data pipelines and model deployment.

Initial POC

Develop a minimal viable product to validate the core concept.

Refinement

Iterate on the POC based on feedback and performance metrics.

Productionizing

Prepare the solution for production, ensuring scalability and reliability.

Maintenance

Monitor performance, address issues, and plan for updates and improvements.
